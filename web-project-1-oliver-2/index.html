What are The Simpsons?

The Simpsons are an American TV show that first aired in 1989, the show still runs today with well over 700 episodes. Overall, it is one of those shows that defined an entire generation with it having from video games to even a movie in 2007. It plays an influencial part in pop culture from their merchandise to eeriely accurrate predictions on future real-world events.

Brief History

The Simpsons was created by Matt Groening and the characters we now know today made their first appearance on April 19, 1987 as a series of shorts on The Tracey Ullman Show. It wasn't until December 17, 1989 where they made their debut on their own TV Series that spun off from the shorts that started 2 years earlier.
